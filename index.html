About Me

Hey there! My name is Gabriel and I'm an odd creature to say the least. I was born in Oakland, California but moved to San Jose, Costa Rica when I was three. After attending kindergarten there, I moved to Tucson, Arizona when I was 5 and stayed there through college. I studied sustainable architecture and city planning at the University of Arizona and graduated with a B.S. in Sustainable Built Environments.

After interning with Tucson's Office of Integrated Planning, I took a 180-degree turn and took a job as an automobile journalist in San Francisco, where I currently reside. I test cars and write about them for a living, but I have an unquenchable thirst for learning and an outright obsession with technology and how it shapes the world. That thirst led me to attend this coding bootcamp where I aim to learn how to code and join the tech industry.
